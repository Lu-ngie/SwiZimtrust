Community Health and Safety

We prioritize the well-being of our community members by implementing comprehensive health and safety measures. Our programs include menstrual health education and awareness campaigns to combat school dropout rates among girls and promote reproductive health. We establish support groups for single mothers and pregnant teens, providing them with the necessary resources and support to navigate their unique challenges. We support child-headed homes with the support they need so that children can go to school without worrying about their survival.

Smart Agriculture and Sustainability

Recognizing the importance of agriculture in livelihoods, we promote smart agricultural practices to enhance food security and income generation. Our initiatives focus on sustainable farming techniques, conservation of natural resources, and climate-smart agriculture to mitigate environmental degradation. By empowering farmers with knowledge and resources, we aim to improve agricultural productivity, enhance resilience to climate change, and promote long-term sustainability in our community.
